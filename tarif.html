Smoothie+ Recipe

Many of us like to start the day off with a big cup of coffee or at least some other form of caffeine such as tea or an energy drink. While we prioritize our caffeine intake, a lot of us also end up skipping what is deemed to be “the most important meal of the day”. With our busy schedules and busy lives, we often don’t spend the times to make our coffee and a healthy breakfast. Lets make the delicious and special Smoothie+!

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • ½ cup oats
  • ½ cup Turkish yogurt
  • 1tsp cinnamon
  • 1tbsp agave (or other sweetener)
  • 1 cup coffee (or double espresso shot)
  • 1 cup milk (or milk substitute)
  • 3 cups ice

To make this smoothie, you will need a banana, oats, yogurt, cinnamon, agave (or your favorite coffee sweetener), milk (or a milk substitute such as almond, soy, oat, or cashew), ice, and, of course, coffee. If you don’t have cold brew, feel free to use whatever coffee you have - you could even do a double espresso shot if that is what you prefer. Just be sure to let your coffee cool before adding it into your smoothie.

Add all the ingredients into your blender and blend until smooth. Pour it into a cup and top it off with a dash of cinnamon. Enjoy!
